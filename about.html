Lewis Beal

I'm a multidisciplinary artist with an interest for human behavior and the phenomena that occur inside and outside our lives. These phenomena are often forgotten so I use my installations as a means to put them back into the spotlight.

I am currently working on the phenomenon of repetition; this can be our daily routines, addictions, or simply the cycle of life or water. To express this, I create cybernetic sculptures that have a repetitive behavior, each one overlooking a different subject.

I have also focused on chain reactions. In the same way, this could be the events that have influenced you as an individual to become who you are today or the evolutionary steps that form a species. For this, I produced a big chain reaction in a dark room with each cybernetic sculpture activating the next, playing with light, sound, smoke, and water within the space.
